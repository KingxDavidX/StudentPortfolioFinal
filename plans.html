My future plans

"We all make choices in life, but in the end our choices make us." -Andrew Ryan From BioShock

I have put a lot of thought into my life after graduation, I have thought of situations ranging from Military service to coding for banks and government contracts. The military has become more and more unappealing as I grow, and I would be lucky to code and work for the government. But what I have become more and more interested in is banking and the financial field as a whole. They pay great and I would be able to do it remotely. I would just have to worry about staying updated with the newest and greatest coding languages. This isn't terrible for me especially because I enjoy learning new coding concepts. I plan on learning for my entire life.

This is all vegue becuase I honestly dont know what the future holds. I just want a job that pays good enough that I can live comfortably and do things I enjoy. If I can do those two things and not have to worry about an unexpected expense making me go into the negative then I will be happy. If a job takes me out of the country or out of the state or anything I will do it. I am not really tied to a state or anyone, I am free to go where I want.
